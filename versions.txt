01/31/2018
cryptostorage.com-v0.0.1-alpha
- First release!
- Create cold storage for multiple cryptocurrencies.
- Print paper wallets for long term storage.
- Runs 100% in your browser so funds are never entrusted to a third party.
- 100% open-source and free to use.  No account necessary.
- Passphrase-protect and split private keys for maximum security.
- Supports BIP38 encryption for Bitcoin and Bitcoin Cash.
- Generate keys securely with automated environment checks.
